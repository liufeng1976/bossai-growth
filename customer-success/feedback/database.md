# BossAI Feedback Database

> 所有用户反馈统一记录在此
> 分类：Bug / UX / Feature / Business / Performance / Pricing / Documentation / AI Quality

---

## 反馈记录模板

```yaml
feedback_id: FB-001
date: YYYY-MM-DD
customer_id: C-001
customer_name: ""
category: bug | ux | feature | business | performance | pricing | documentation | ai_quality
severity: critical | major | minor | suggestion
status: new | acknowledged | in_progress | resolved | declined
source: wechat | email | in-app | interview | other
description: ""
expected_behavior: ""
actual_behavior: ""
related_feature: ""
attachments: []
notes: ""
resolved_at: ""
```

## 使用说明

1. 每条反馈创建一个记录，编号递增 FB-001, FB-002...
2. Critical 级别的 Bug 24 小时内必须响应
3. 每周在 weekly-report 中汇总新增反馈
4. Feature Request 进入 V1.1 Priority 评估
