# BossAI Pricing

## Model

One-time purchase, lifetime use. Bring your own API keys. Pay providers directly.

## Plans

### Starter — $29
For individual creators. Single device. Full BossAI Desktop features. 10 free trials. Free V1.x updates.

### Professional — $79 (Recommended)
For professional creators and team leads. All Starter features. Business Context. Provider Profiles. Workflow Templates. Priority support.

### Business — $299
For businesses and studios. All Professional features. Multi-device. Team collaboration. Enterprise support.

### Enterprise — Custom
For large organizations. Private deployment. SLA. Custom development. Dedicated CS.

## FAQ

**Who pays API costs?** You do. Pay DeepSeek, OpenAI, etc. directly.

**Free trial?** Yes. 10 free generations, no activation required.

**Pay again after purchase?** V1.x updates free. V2 upgrade sold separately.
