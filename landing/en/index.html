How is BossAI different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a chat interface. BossAI is a content workspace. One sentence creates copy, images, and video in one tool. No switching between apps.

Do I need my own API keys?

Yes. BossAI uses your own API keys. We do not provide models or resell tokens. DeepSeek offers 5M free tokens on signup to get started.

Is my data safe?

All data stays on your computer. API keys are stored locally. BossAI never uploads your data or keys. Local-first by design.

Can I try before buying?

Yes. 10 free trial generations are built in. No activation code needed, no feature limits. Purchase a license when you are ready.

What is the Energy Pass?

Desktop is a permanent license that never expires. Professional and Business include a 1-Year Energy Pass that can be renewed yearly. Energy Pass does not affect your data, your local files, or your permanent software license. BossAI remains BYO AI — we do not provide free APIs or free tokens.

Any payments after purchase?

Desktop is a one-time purchase for lifetime use. V1.x updates are free. V2 upgrade sold separately. You only pay AI provider API costs directly to them.
