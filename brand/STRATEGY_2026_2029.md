# BossAI 2026–2029 Strategy

**Status:** Strategic Plan  
**Planning Cycle:** 2026–2029  

This document defines the long-term strategic direction of BossAI.

---

## Stage 1 (2026): Validate

**Primary Product:** BossAI Desktop  
**Mission:** Validate the business model.  
**Goals:** Launch V1, acquire first 100 paying customers, build customer success process, complete BYO AI product positioning.  
**Success Criteria:** Customers are willing to pay for BossAI software value — not for AI Tokens.

## Stage 2 (2027): Expand

**Products:** BossAI Desktop, BossAI Cloud  
**Mission:** Expand from individual users to teams.  
**Goals:** Team collaboration, shared projects, cloud synchronization, organization management, subscription revenue.  
**Success Criteria:** Recurring revenue exceeds one-time license revenue.

## Stage 3 (2028): Platform

**Mission:** Become an AI Work Platform.  
**Products:** Marketplace, AI Workers, Workflow Store, Industry Packs, Connector Store.  
**Goals:** Allow third parties to build on BossAI.  
**Success Criteria:** External developers contribute value to the ecosystem.

## Stage 4 (2029): Ecosystem

**Mission:** Become the operating platform connecting businesses with every AI capability.  
**Goals:** Global expansion, enterprise ecosystem, partner network, education, certification, marketplace economy.

## Strategic Principles

BossAI never competes with AI model companies. BossAI grows as AI becomes stronger. BossAI remains provider agnostic. BossAI remains customer first. BossAI remains work first.

## North Star

BossAI helps businesses complete more work with less effort.

## Success Indicators

2026: 100 paying customers. 2027: 1,000 paying customers. 2028: 10,000 active businesses. 2029: Global AI Work Platform.

## Final Statement

BossAI is not built for one AI generation. BossAI is built for the next decade of AI.
