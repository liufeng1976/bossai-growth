# BossAI CEO Declaration V1.0

**Status:** Active  
**Effective Date:** Immediately  

---

BossAI Foundation 1.0 is now officially complete. The company's product architecture, business model, governance system, GTM framework and long-term strategy have reached the planned milestone. This marks the end of the Foundation phase. From today forward, BossAI enters its next stage.

## Phase 2: Go To Market

Our priority is no longer building more software. Our priority is helping real customers complete real work. Success is measured by customer value — not feature count, not provider count, not commit count.

## Product Policy

BossAI Desktop V1.0 is frozen. Only the following work is allowed: P0 / P1 bug fixes, stability improvements, UX improvements, onboarding improvements, and customer-requested enhancements. No large-scale feature expansion without customer evidence.

## Product Development Rule

Every new feature must originate from customer evidence. The development sequence is Customer Feedback → Evidence → Candidate → Roadmap → Sprint → Release. Ideas alone are never sufficient.

## Company Focus

For the next 90 days, the company focuses on five objectives: acquire the first paying customers, build successful customer case studies, reduce Time To First Success (TTFS), improve website conversion, and prepare V1.1 only when validated by customers.

## Long-term Principle

BossAI will never chase AI models. BossAI will continue helping businesses complete work regardless of which AI model becomes the industry leader. AI changes. Customer work remains. BossAI is built around work — not models.

## Success Definition

BossAI succeeds when customers open BossAI every day to complete their work. Not because it supports more AI providers. Not because it has more features. But because it helps them finish today's work.

## Final Statement

Foundation is complete. Now we build the company. From Build → To Sell → To Learn → To Improve → To Scale. BossAI enters its commercial era today.
