# BossAI Business Constitution V1.0

**Status:** Active  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** BossAI  

This document defines the long-term business principles of BossAI. All pricing, sales, partnerships, roadmap and future products must follow these principles.

---

## 1. Software Company

BossAI is a software company. Not an AI model company. Not an API reseller. Not a Token reseller. BossAI sells software value.

## 2. BYO AI

Desktop always follows Bring Your Own AI, Bring Your Own API Key. Users own their AI accounts. BossAI never forces users to purchase AI services through BossAI.

## 3. Customer Ownership

Customers own their projects, assets, templates, business data, and AI keys. BossAI does not lock customer data. Data export should always be supported.

## 4. Vendor Independence

BossAI never depends on a single AI provider. Every provider can be replaced, disabled, or added. No vendor lock-in.

## 5. Revenue Model

Desktop is one-time license. Cloud is subscription. Marketplace is revenue sharing. Enterprise is annual contract. Professional services are optional.

## 6. Customer First

Every feature must answer: Does this create customer value? Not: Does this look technically impressive?

## 7. AI Evolution

AI models will continue to change. BossAI should never depend on one model. BossAI should survive every model generation.

## 8. Marketplace Economy

Future value comes from AI Workers, Workflow, Templates, Connectors, Business Packs, and Industry Packs. Not from model APIs.

## 9. Global Strategy

BossAI is designed as a global product. Support Chinese, English, and future multilingual. Desktop first, Cloud second, Mobile third.

## 10. Long-term Goal

BossAI aims to become the operating platform connecting businesses with every AI capability. The business should become stronger as AI models improve — not weaker.
