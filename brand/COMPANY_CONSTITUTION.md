# BossAI Company Constitution V1.0

**Status:** Active  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** BossAI  

This Constitution is the highest-level principle for all BossAI products, engineering, marketing, sales and future business decisions. If any future feature, UI, roadmap or marketing conflicts with this Constitution, the product should change — not the Constitution.

---

## Mission

Help every business work with AI instead of working for AI. BossAI exists to organize AI, automate work, and improve productivity. BossAI does not replace human decision-making. BossAI helps people execute ideas.

## Vision

Become the world's AI Work Operating Platform. Not another AI model. Not another chatbot. Not another prompt tool. A platform that allows every AI service to work together.

## Core Principles

**Work First.** BossAI is designed around completing work. Users care about finished work, not which model generated it. Every feature must answer one question: "Does it help users finish work faster?"

**BYO AI (Bring Your Own AI).** Users own their AI providers. Users choose their own API Keys. BossAI never forces users to use a specific model. BossAI Desktop does not resell Tokens. BossAI earns revenue from software value — not AI consumption.

**Provider Agnostic.** BossAI supports any AI provider. DeepSeek, OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, MiniMax, Runway, Veo, and future providers. No vendor lock-in.

**Local First.** User data belongs to users. Desktop projects stay on local devices by default. Cloud services must be optional. Privacy is a feature.

**Human First.** Humans decide. BossAI executes. AI assists. People remain responsible for final decisions.

## Product Architecture

BossAI Desktop → BossAI Cloud → BossAI Platform → Marketplace → AI Workers. Each layer builds on the previous one. No product should duplicate another.

## Commercial Model

Desktop is one-time purchase, Bring Your Own AI, local execution. Cloud is subscription, team collaboration, managed AI services. Marketplace includes templates, workflows, connectors, and AI Workers.

## User Experience Principles

Never expose technical complexity unless users request it. Default experience is simple, business-oriented, and result-oriented. Advanced users can access professional settings.

## Marketing Principles

Never sell technology. Sell business outcomes. Never lead with models, providers, architecture, or capabilities. Lead with problems solved, time saved, revenue created, and efficiency improved.

## Engineering Principles

Keep architecture modular. Avoid vendor lock-in. Avoid duplicated implementations. Design for long-term maintainability. Every module should have a single responsibility.

## Success Metrics

BossAI is measured by active users, paid customers, customer retention, time saved, and customer satisfaction — not by number of AI providers, number of features, or lines of code.

## Company Motto

Chinese: 老板负责决策。BossAI 负责执行。  
English: You Decide. BossAI Delivers.

---

This Constitution applies to every BossAI product today and in the future.
